# Sample Emergency Quiz - Google Form Guide

## How to Create the Quiz

1. **Go to Google Forms**: https://forms.google.com
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Create a new form**
4. **Title**: "Emergency Preparedness Quiz"
5. **Description**: "Test your knowledge on emergency procedures and safety"

---

## Sample Questions (Copy these into your form)

### Question 1: Multiple Choice
**Question**: What should you do FIRST when an earthquake occurs indoors?

**Options**:
- [ ] Run outside immediately
- [ ] Drop, Cover, and Hold On
- [ ] Stand in a doorway
- [ ] Use the elevator to exit the building

**Correct Answer**: Drop, Cover, and Hold On

---

### Question 2: Multiple Choice
**Question**: In case of a fire, what should you do if you encounter smoke?

**Options**:
- [ ] Run through the smoke
- [ ] Stay low and crawl
- [ ] Hold your breath and walk fast
- [ ] Close your eyes and walk

**Correct Answer**: Stay low and crawl

---

### Question 3: Multiple Choice
**Question**: When reporting an emergency, what information is MOST important?

**Options**:
- [ ] Your name and address
- [ ] The type of emergency and location
- [ ] The time of day
- [ ] Weather conditions

**Correct Answer**: The type of emergency and location

---

### Question 4: Checkbox (Select all that apply)
**Question**: Which items should be in an emergency preparedness kit? (Select all that apply)

**Options**:
- [ ] Flashlight and batteries
- [ ] First aid kit
- [ ] Non-perishable food
- [ ] Water (at least 3 days supply)
- [ ] All of the above

**Correct Answer**: All of the above

---

### Question 5: Multiple Choice
**Question**: What is the emergency phone number to call in most countries?

**Options**:
- [ ] 911
- [ ] 112
- [ ] 999
- [ ] Depends on the country (all above may be correct)

**Correct Answer**: Depends on the country (all above may be correct)

---

### Question 6: Short Answer
**Question**: What does the acronym "RACE" stand for in fire safety?

**Expected Answer**: Rescue, Alarm, Contain, Evacuate (or similar)

---

### Question 7: Multiple Choice
**Question**: During an emergency evacuation, you should:

**Options**:
- [ ] Use elevators
- [ ] Take personal belongings first
- [ ] Use stairs and exit calmly
- [ ] Wait for others to leave first

**Correct Answer**: Use stairs and exit calmly

---

### Question 8: True/False
**Question**: You should only report emergencies during business hours.

**Options**:
- [ ] True
- [ ] False

**Correct Answer**: False

## Form Settings

1. Click the **Settings** (gear icon) at the top
2. Enable **"Collect email addresses"** (optional, for tracking)
3. Enable **"Limit to 1 response"** (optional, prevents multiple submissions)
4. Click **"See responses"** tab to view results later

---

## How to Get the Shareable Link

1. Click the **"Send"** button (top right)
2. Click the **"Link"** icon (chain link symbol)
3. Click **"Shorten URL"** (optional)
4. **Copy the link**
5. Paste this link into the **"Google Form Quiz URL"** field in your admin panel

---

## Example Link Format

Your Google Form link will look like:
```
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/[FORM_ID]/viewform
```

Or if shortened:
```
https://forms.gle/[SHORT_CODE]
```
